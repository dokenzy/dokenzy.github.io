  1. getmap: Downloading maps from Open- StreetMap, Google Maps or Google Street View
  2. ghsystem: to typeset all the hazard and precautionary state- ments and pictograms in a very easy way.
  3. glossaries: to assist generating lists of terms, symbols or abbreviations (glossaries).
  4. Roboto: an open type family designed for high resolution mobile devices
  5. dataref: The dataref is here to help you with managing your data points.
  6. bloX: an English translation of the French schemabloc package for making block diagrams in LATEX2e
  7. ExSol: to allow embedding exercises and solu- tions in the LATEX source
  8. reflectgraphics: adds fancy reflections at the bottom of graphics
  9. gitinfo2: A package for accessing metadata from the git dvcs
